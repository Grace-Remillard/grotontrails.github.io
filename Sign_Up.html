Email List Sign Up

The Friends of the Groton Trails Network maintain an email list:

Join this list to learn about and discuss the trails of Groton including activities, work parties, and trails advocacy. We will use this list to notify you of our guided hikes, bike rides, ski or snowshoe events, and similar excursions. When we organize work parties we will also post a call for volunteers using this list.

As a group member you can post questions and answers on your favorite trail, trail conditions (e.g. "there is a tree down on the XYZ trail"), if you have suggestions for a trail reroute around a wet spot, or similar.

To join please send an email with your full name to gtc@grotontrails.org, and we will send you an invitation. This way we can keep spammers away.
