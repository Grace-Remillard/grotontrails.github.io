The Groton Trails crew maintains 2 email lists:


Groton Trails Activities email list:

Join this list to learn about and discuss the trails of Groton including activities and trails advocacy. We will use this list to notify you of our guided hikes, and similar excursions. As a group member you can post questions and answers on your favorite trail, trail conditions (e.g. "there is a tree down on the XYZ trail"), if you have suggestions for a trail reroute around a wet spot, or similar. To join please send an email with your full name to gtc@grotontrails.org, and we will send you an invitation. This way we can keep spammers away.


Activities email list home page

http://groups.google.com/group/groton-trails?hl=en&lnk=


Groton Trails Volunteers email list:

There are over a hundred trails in town, and without our dedicated volunteers we would not be able to keep them open. If you like to be informed about our trail building or maintenance events, please subscribe. This is an announcement-only list.
