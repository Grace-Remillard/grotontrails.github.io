Printable Trail Maps

There are over 100 trails throughout the Town of Groton.  Some of the trails work their way through Groton Conservation Trust properties while others are owned by the town.  There are also rights of way that cross privately owned property that provide access to trails.  In all instances we ask that you be respectful of the trails and those that live near these areas. 

Trail Use Guidelines
  • Hiking, horseback riding, nature study, and other low-impact activities are welcome throughout the trail network
  • All motorized use is prohibited
  • No hunting on most trails (look for signage and wear blaze orange during hunting season on trails that permit hunting) Visit Hunting, Fishing, and Trapping Seasons for the state hunting season calendar.
  • No littering or dumping
  • No fires
  • No camping
  • No alcoholic beverages
  • Do not feed, approach or touch wildlife, including waterfowl
  • Take personal protection measures to reduce your exposure to ticks and mosquito bites
  • Please pick up after your dog and properly dispose of the waste
