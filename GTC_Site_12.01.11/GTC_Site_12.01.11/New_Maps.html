Trails

 

In addition to the maps included as pdf files on this site we are pleased to present new trail maps using current GPS and mapping programs.  Groton resident, Chris Marsh, has done a yeoman’s job assembling the trail maps accessible at the links below.  Chris has taken on this endeavor to assist him in completing his Eagle Scout project and we are all grateful for his work. 


Click on the map below and a new window will open that will allow you to see the available trails across Groton that have been mapped in the new format.  Next click on the hiker icon denoting a mapped trail for the trail that most interests you.  A detailed trail map will then be visible for you. 


As we map additional trails in town we will add the appropriate maps and links for you to enjoy.
